ABOUT

HISTORY

On April 18, 2017 Villa Tuna Republika first branch was established in Butuan city. It was inspired by a former colleague's business to bring a healthier food option to the market.It was only in March 2021 in the midst of the pandemic that the partners decided the need to expand the business. Its main purpose is to provide job opportunities to those who lost their jobs during the crisis. The second branch was located at Gingoog City, and within the span of 1 year another 4 branches were opened in the neighboring cities.As the business proceeded with the warm welcome and acceptance of the buying public, the business partners agreed to make it a corporation giving birth to Villa Tuna Foods Corp. Villa Tuna Foods Corp envisions to be the fastest-growing fast-casual tuna restaurant Serving fresh delicious tuna Dishes in the Philippines.

MISSION

Our Mission is to provide fast, healthy, affordable, and delicious high-quality tuna dishes with excellent customer service to the market.

VISION

To be the fastest growing fast-casual tun restaurant, developing a healthy working environment and encouraging both financial and professional growth to every employee. To share the benefits and profits not only to its business partners but also to its employees.
